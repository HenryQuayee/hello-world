A timeline of Susan B. Anthony's Life

  • 1820: Born in Adams, Massachusetts
  • 1848: Joined the Daughters of Temperance
  • 1851: Met Elizabeth Cady Stanton
  • 1852: Founded the Women's New York State Temperance Society
  • 1863: Founded the Women's Loyal National League
  • 1860-1870: Published the weekly Revolution
  • 1869: Founded the National Women Suffrage Association
  • 1872: Arrested and stood trial for illegally voting in a national election
  • 1876: Presented a woman's Declaration of Rights
  • 1888: Founded the International Council of Women
  • 1895-1896: Campaigned in California for women's right to vote
  • 1904: Founded the International Women Suffrage Alliance
  • 1906: Died in Rochester, NY
